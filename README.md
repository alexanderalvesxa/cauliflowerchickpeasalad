# Indian-Spiced Cauliflower & Chickpea Salad Recipe

Looking for a fresh, vibrant, and protein-packed salad with a flavorful
twist? This **Indian-Spiced Cauliflower & Chickpea Salad** is a
delicious vegetarian main dish, combining the warm spices of curry with
the zesty freshness of lime, ginger, and parsley. Perfect for a light
lunch or a wholesome dinner, it's as nutritious as it is satisfying.

This salad is a delightful fusion of roasted vegetables and aromatic
Indian spices. The combination of **cauliflower, chickpeas, and
carrots** roasted to perfection provides a comforting texture, while the
**yogurt-lime dressing** adds a creamy and tangy balance. Optional
jalapeño gives a subtle kick for spice lovers. Plus, it's a quick and
easy recipe, ready in just **40 minutes**!

**Ingredients:**

-   1 tablespoon curry powder
-   1 tablespoon olive oil
-   ¼ teaspoon salt
-   1 ½ cups cauliflower florets
-   1 cup canned no-salt-added garbanzo beans (chickpeas), rinsed and
    drained
-   ¾ cup ½-inch carrot slices
-   ¼ cup plain fat-free yogurt
-   1 tablespoon lime juice
-   ½ teaspoon black pepper
-   ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger (or ¼ teaspoon ground ginger)
-   ½ teaspoon minced fresh jalapeño chile pepper (optional)
-   1 tablespoon fat-free milk (optional, for thinning dressing)
-   2 cups torn red-tipped leaf lettuce
-   1 cup packed fresh Italian parsley
-   ¼ cup thinly sliced red onion

**Instructions:**

1.  **Preheat the Oven:** Preheat your oven to **450°F (230°C)**.
2.  **Prepare the Vegetables:** In a medium bowl, mix **curry powder,
    olive oil, and salt**. Toss in the **cauliflower, chickpeas, and
    carrot slices** until evenly coated. Spread the mixture in a
    **15x10x1-inch baking pan**.
3.  **Roast:** Roast for **20--25 minutes** or until the vegetables are
    tender, stirring once halfway through.
4.  **Make the Dressing:** In a small bowl, whisk together **yogurt,
    lime juice, ginger**, and **jalapeño** (if using). Thin with milk if
    a looser consistency is desired.
5.  **Assemble the Salad:** In a large bowl, combine the roasted
    vegetables, **lettuce, parsley, and red onion**. Pour the dressing
    over the salad and toss gently to coat.
6.  **Serve:** Serve immediately as a nutritious and filling main-dish
    salad.

**Nutrition (Per Serving):**

-   Calories: 241
-   Fat: 9g
-   Carbohydrates: 33g
-   Protein: 11g

**Tips & Variations:**

-   **Add more spice:** Increase jalapeño or sprinkle chili powder for
    extra heat.
-   **Make it a meal:** Serve with warm naan or your favorite [taco
    pasta](https://goodhoodrecipes.com/taco-pasta-recipe/) for a hearty
    fusion dinner.
-   **Meal prep:** Store roasted vegetables and dressing separately for
    up to 2 days to keep the lettuce crisp.

This **Indian-Spiced Cauliflower & Chickpea Salad** is perfect for
anyone seeking a wholesome, flavorful, and plant-based meal. Whether for
a quick weekday lunch or a light dinner, it brings a burst of taste and
nutrition in every bite.
